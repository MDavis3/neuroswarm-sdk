# Sources and Attribution

This project is an original software implementation built from public sources for
educational and portfolio purposes. It is inspired by the Neuro-SWARM3 work from
Dr. Ali Yanik's group and related publications/patents.

Primary sources:
- Neil Hardy, Ahsan Habib, Tanya Ivanov, and Ahmet Ali Yanik,
  "Neuro-SWARM3: System-on-a-Nanoparticle for Wireless Recording of Brain Activity,"
  IEEE Photonics Technology Letters, vol. 33, no. 16, 2021. DOI: 10.1109/LPT.2021.3092780

- "System and Method for Wireless Recording of Brain Activity," U.S. patent publication,
  published June 13, 2022 (related to Neuro-SWARM3 nanoparticle probes).
